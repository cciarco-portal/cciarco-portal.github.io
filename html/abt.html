Overview

This CCIARCO PORTAL is prepared by Corporate Communications, Internal Audit, Risk Management, and Compliance Office (CCIARCO) as part of an effort to develop documentation of business processes and internal controls that support continuous improvement.

The following are benefits of having effective documentation:

  • (a) Ability to maintain and improve system implementation with clear and easy-to-follow procedures;
  • (b) Know where to find procedures and records accordingly;
  • (c) Increased transparency and accountability of roles and responsibilities;
  • (d) Quality improvement and systematization across all management systems.

Reputation that’s Built Inside–Out

Our goal is to cultivate a culture of trustworthiness and ethical conduct that naturally leads to a positive reputation.

  • Delivering quality products and services;
  • Addressing underlying issues and practices; and
  • Consistently demonstrating trustworthiness and ethical conduct internally.

Accountability–Based Governance

Our goal is to ensure that everyone takes responsibility for what they do, helping the business run smoothly and building trust with customers and partners.

  • Ensuring systems and processes are in place;
  • Defining roles and responsibilities;
  • Monitoring adherence to policies, processes, and procedures;
  • Reviewing and reporting performance;
  • Emphasizing accountability; and
  • Driving overall effectiveness and integrity within the organization.

Objective Assurance to Business Value

Our goal is to provide unbiased feedback to ensure we are doing things right and adding value to the business.

  • Conducting independent evaluations and assessments to ensure systems and practices are aligned with business goals.

CCIARCO is taking care of You!

By focusing on the key areas that help the business succeed by:
  • Keeping customers happy
  • Making good products and services
  • Taking care of employees
  • Managing money wisely
